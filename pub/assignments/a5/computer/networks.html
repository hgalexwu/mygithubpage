Networks

A network is a groupd of two or more computers linked together. There many types of networks:

  • local-area network (LAN): where the computers are physically close together.
  • wide-area network (WAN): where the computers are farther apart and connected via telephone lines or radio waves.
  • campus-area networks (CAN): where the computers are within a limited geographic area, such as a campus or military base.
  • home-are networks (HAN): which is a network contained within a users home that connects a person digitals devices.
  • metropolitan-area networks(MAN): a data network designed for a town or city.
